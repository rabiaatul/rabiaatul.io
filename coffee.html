Coffee Series

Iced Americano

Bold and robust espresso diluted with hot water, offering a rich, full-bodied flavor.

Iced Latte

Smooth and refreshing espresso blended with cold milk and ice – perfect for warm days.

Iced Spanish Latte

A rich blend of espresso, chilled milk, and a touch of sweetened condensed milk for a creamy, smooth finish.

Iced Mocha

Bold espresso blended with cold milk and chocolate syrup, served over ice for a refreshing, sweet boost.

Iced Strawberry Latte

A creative mix of espresso, milk, and real strawberry syrup – fruity, creamy, and energizing.

Iced Mint Latte

Cool mint flavor meets espresso and milk over ice – a crisp twist on the classic iced latte.

Iced Buttercream Latte

Velvety espresso and milk infused with sweet buttercream syrup – indulgent and smooth.

Iced Caramel Machiato

Layers of espresso and milk over ice, topped with luscious caramel drizzle for a rich flavor experience.

Iced Aren Sugar Latte

Espresso and creamy milk sweetened with natural aren (palm) sugar for a toasty, earthy sweetness.

Hot Americano

Smooth espresso diluted with hot water to deliver a clean, full-bodied cup with a mellow bite.

Hot Latte

Classic espresso topped with steamed milk and a light layer of foam – creamy, cozy, and balanced.

Cappucino

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and thick foam – a bold yet airy coffee experience.

Blanc Brew

A specialty café offering artisanal coffee, handcrafted drinks, and warm ambiance across Malaysia.
