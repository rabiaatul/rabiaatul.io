The Story of Blanc & Brew

Blanc & Brew began in 2020 as a quiet escape from the city buzz — a café where simplicity met soul, and every cup of coffee was brewed with meaning. Inspired by Scandinavian minimalism and Malaysia’s vibrant café culture, our founders envisioned a calm, elegant space where people could slow down, reflect, and reconnect — with themselves and each other.

What started as a humble space in Kuala Lumpur soon grew into a nationwide café experience. Today, Blanc & Brew can be found in some of Malaysia’s most beloved locations — including Paradigm Mall in Johor Bahru, Ipoh Parade Mall in Perak, Mid Valley Megamall in Kuala Lumpur, Sunway Pyramid in Selangor, AEON Mall in Melaka, and Kuantan City Mall in Pahang. Each outlet carries the same warm, minimalist design, rich coffee aroma, and peaceful energy that defines who we are.
