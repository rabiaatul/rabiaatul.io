Pizza Series

Mozzarella Pizza

A classic favorite topped with fresh mozzarella, tomato sauce, and basil on a thin, wood-fired crust—simple, cheesy, and authentically Italian.

Pepperoni Pizza

Crispy-edged beef pepperoni layered over rich tomato sauce and melted mozzarella—bold, savory, and irresistibly satisfying.

Salami Pizza

A flavorful twist on the classic, featuring spicy chicken salami atop a vibrant pesto sauce base, topped with melted mozzarella—herby, savory, and perfectly balanced on a thin, wood-fired crust.

BBQ Smoke Chicken Pizza

Tender halal smoked chicken in sweet and smoky BBQ sauce, layered with mozzarella and red onions—rich, bold, and perfectly balanced.

Olive Garlic Pizza

A savory mix of black olives, roasted garlic, and mozzarella on a tomato base—earthy, aromatic, and a delight for Mediterranean flavor lovers.

Truffle Pizza

Luxuriously topped with real truffle shavings and drizzled with authentic truffle oil, layered over creamy mozzarella and mushrooms—an earthy, aromatic masterpiece on a perfectly thin, wood-fired crust.
