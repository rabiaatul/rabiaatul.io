Pasta Series

Carbonara

Classic creamy pasta with halal smoky beef bacon, parmesan cheese, and egg yolk—rich, silky, and deeply comforting in every bite.

Spaghetti Bolognese

A hearty Italian-style meat sauce made with slow-cooked minced beef and tomato, served over al dente spaghetti—flavorful and satisfying.

Aglio E Olio

A fragrant and flavorful dish featuring spaghetti tossed in garlic-infused olive oil, chili flakes, and fresh parsley, topped with juicy, pan-seared prawns for a savory seafood twist on a classic Italian favorite.

Arrabiata Seafood Pasta

A spicy symphony of ocean treasures tossed in bold arrabiata sauce—fiery, zesty, and made to thrill your taste buds.

Pesto Pasta

Fragrant basil pesto blended with olive oil, pine nuts, and parmesan, coating each strand of spaghetti with vibrant, herbaceous flavor.

Spicy Cajun Chicken Pasta

Tender, seasoned chicken in a spicy Cajun cream sauce over penne pasta—bold, creamy, and full of zesty Southern-style heat.

Vegetarian Pasta Salad

A wholesome and refreshing salad made with pasta, crisp seasonal vegetables, and a light, zesty vinaigrette—colorful, meat-free, and perfect as a nourishing light meal or side dish.

Lasagna

Layers of pasta sheets, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese baked together—hearty, cheesy, and classic Italian comfort.

Mac and Cheese

Creamy, cheesy macaroni baked to golden perfection—comforting, rich, and irresistibly gooey with every forkful.
